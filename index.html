Hello, from Blushé Artistry!

I'm Zoané Cilliers, the heart and hands behind Blushé Artistry. As a professional makeup artist, my passion lies in helping women feel confident, radiant, and undeniably beautiful — inside and out.

Blushé Artistry was created with a simple mission: to celebrate and enhance every woman’s natural beauty through the transformative power of makeup. Whether it's for a special occasion, a moment of self-care, or one of the most important days of your life — your wedding day — I’m here to ensure you look and feel your absolute best.

With a calm, personal approach and a deep love for what I do, I bring artistry, attention to detail, and genuine care to every face I work with. Because I truly believe that when you feel beautiful, it shows in everything you do. Thank you for being here — I can’t wait to be part of your beauty journey.
